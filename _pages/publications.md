**Enhancing Nuclear Reactor Core Simulation through Data-Based Surrogate Models** (2025)<br>
with Alain Grossetête & Nicolas Vayatis<br>
*This paper develops a ML surrogate simulation scheme for fast integration of Differential Algebraic Equations modeling a nuclear reactor core.*<br>

[PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.16148)

**Lipo+: Frugal global optimization for lipschitz functions** (2024)<br>
with Gaëtan Serré, Sophia Chirrane, Argyris Kalogeratos & Nicolas Vayatis<br>
*This paper develops two major improvements on the global optimization LIPO from <a href="https://proceedings.mlr.press/v70/malherbe17a/malherbe17a.pdf">Malherbe & Vayatis, 2017</a>: an empirical stopping criterion and a decaying exploration rate.*<br>
*SETN 2024*<br>
[PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2406.19723)
